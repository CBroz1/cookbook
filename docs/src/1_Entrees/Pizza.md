# Title

## Shopping

- Produce
    - bell pepper (1)
    - mushrooms
- Dairy
    - mozzarella cheese (8oz)
    - parmesan cheese (2tbsp)
    - ricotta cheese (200g)
- Middle
    - pizza sauce (1c)
    - pizza dough
    - other pizza toppings
- Staples

## Prep

1. Slice veg
    1. bell pepper
    1. mushrooms
1. Slice mozzarella
1. Grate parm

## Cook

1. Thaw dough
1. Oven to 500F
1. Roll out dough
1. Add sauce, toppings, cheese
1. Bake 8-12m

## Notes

- Suggested Time: 25m
    - Prep: 15m
    - Cook: 10m
- Servings: 4
