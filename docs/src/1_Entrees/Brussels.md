# Brussels

## Shopping

- Produce
    - Brussels sprouts
- Dairy
    - Parmesan
- Middle
    - Panchetta
- Staples

## Prep

- Preheat oven to 425-450°
- Cut bottom and halves
- Ditch outer dirty leaves
- Coat in olive oil and salt
- Arrange face down

## Cook

- Cook pancetta like pan bacon
- Bake 425-450° 39m
- Combine all in bowl, add parm

## Notes

- Suggested Time: 50m
    - Prep: 10m
    - Cook: 40m
